about us

We understand that Organizational Charts are hard to make,share and maintian and we strongly believe that Organizational Charts should be more then boxes and lines without any interactive element.

With Organimi get richer data on people with current projects, skills and social information.

Organimi can also be used as an interactive office directory so you don't have to dig through lists of people just to find the right one.
